Pesto Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pesto
  • Grated parmesan

Preparation

  1. Boil water in medium pot
  2. Add spaghetti to pot and cook until desired consistency
  3. Strain spaghetti and return it to pot
  4. Add pesto to pot and mix well
  5. Serve on plate
  6. Grate parmesan over the top
