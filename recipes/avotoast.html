Avocado Toast Recipe

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Avocado
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. Toast two slices of bread in toaster
  2. Cut avocado in half
  3. Remove pit and peel off skin
  4. Place each half on a peice of bread
  5. Lightly smush the avocado with a fork
  6. Top with salt and pepper
